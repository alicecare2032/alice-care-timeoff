Time to Market Trend Analysis - GL Ready Process Impact

What this chart shows:

This chart displays average program duration by fiscal quarter. Each data point represents the mean number of days (Kickoff to GA) for all programs that started in that quarter.

The Trendline (dashed gray line) is a linear regression fitted across ALL quarters, showing the overall direction and slope of improvement over time. A downward slope indicates programs are generally getting faster.

Quarterly average program duration - demonstrating standardization effectiveness

Trend Insights & Analysis

Understanding these metrics:

"Improvement Since GL Ready Launch" compares just TWO data points: the baseline quarter when GL Ready launched (FY2025 Q1) versus the most recent quarter. It answers: "How much faster/slower are we now compared to when we started?"

This differs from the Trendline in the chart, which uses ALL quarters to calculate a statistical trend. The trendline shows overall trajectory; the Improvement metric shows specific before/after comparison.
