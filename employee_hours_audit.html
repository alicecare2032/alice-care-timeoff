Personal Attendant Payroll Breakdown

Personal Attendant Overtime Rules (CA Labor Code §1451)

Overtime (1.5x): After 9 hours/day OR 40 hours/week  |  No Double Time: Personal Attendants are not entitled to double time  |  Holiday Pay (1.5x): Company recognized holidays  |  Workweek: Monday 12:01 AM through Sunday midnight

Time Calculations

Regular (R): Actual worked time from clock-in to clock-out (columns E & F)  |  Non Worked (TMN): Scheduled time minus actual worked time (when caregiver works less than scheduled)  |  OT Calculation: Only Regular hours count toward overtime thresholds - TMN hours are excluded

Overnight Shift Splitting

When a shift crosses midnight, hours are split between days. Hours before midnight count for the start date, hours after midnight count for the next day. Post-midnight hours that fall into the next week are excluded from this week's report.
● Orange end time = ends next day  |  ● Red end time = ends next week  |  ● Blue start time = continued from previous day

Note: Cancel Credit Hours

Cancel credit hours for cancellations are not reported in this audit. The source data excludes cancelled shifts, so we cannot determine cancellation timing relative to the 24-hour policy. The actual payroll report (not this high-level audit) contains the complete cancel credit calculations.
