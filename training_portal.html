Knowledge Check

Important: You must answer all questions correctly (100%) to complete this training. Take your time and review the video content if needed.

1 A caregiver can perform a medical task if the family signs a consent form. True or False
Correct! CDSS does not authorize medical services for Registered Home Care Aides and consent does not change that.
2 Which actions fit CDSS rules when a client self-administers medication? Select all that apply
Correct! CDSS allows limited help that supports self-administration, not medication administration.
3 Which task is medical and NOT allowed? Select one best answer
Correct! CDSS lists monitoring or recording blood pressure as a medical service.
4 A client asks you to check their blood sugar. What do you do? Select one best answer
Correct! CDSS lists glucose readings as a medical service and does not authorize it for caregivers.
5 A client asks for an insulin shot. What is the correct response? Select one best answer
Correct! CDSS lists diabetic insulin shots as medical services.
6 What do you do first when asked to perform a medical task? Select one best answer
Correct! Your first step is a clear refusal and rule statement, then escalation to Alice Care.
