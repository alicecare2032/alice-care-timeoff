Non-Medical Home Care Services

CDSS Licensed Home Care Organization #344700080

Alice.care provides non-medical home care services as defined by the California Department of Social Services (CDSS) under Health and Safety Code § 1796.12. Our Registered Home Care Aides assist clients who, because of advanced age or physical or mental disability, need support with the following:

Bathing Dressing Personal Hygiene & Grooming Toileting & Incontinence Care Feeding Meal Planning & Preparation Housekeeping Laundry Transportation Shopping Companionship Ambulating Transferring Positioning Exercising Correspondence Telephone Calls Medication Reminders
Medication Assistance: Our caregivers may open medication bottles, canisters, or blister packs, remind clients to take prescribed medications, and remind clients to refill medications. Caregivers assist only with medications the client self-administers.

Services We Do Not Provide

Medical services require licensed healthcare professionals

Registered Home Care Aides do not provide medical services. CDSS does not grant waivers or authorizations allowing Home Care Organizations to provide medical services. The following are examples of services our caregivers cannot perform (This is not an exhaustive list. Additional restrictions may apply):

Medication Administration Injections Glucose readings (blood sugar) Blood Pressure Monitoring Vital Signs Recording Wound Care Catheter Care Medical Device Management Tube Feedings Physical therapy Colostomy bag care Enemas IV insertions
Please Note: If your care needs include medical services, our caregivers are instructed to refuse and cannot provide medical services. We recommend consulting with a licensed home health agency or your healthcare provider to arrange appropriate support.
