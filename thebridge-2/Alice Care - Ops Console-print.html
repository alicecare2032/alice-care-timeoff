Operations console — a design exploration
The current console is a dense React-Bootstrap grid with raw log output, colored severity text, and little visual hierarchy. This exploration keeps every function and every data field in place — operators can still scan events, triage delays, assign providers, manage clients & providers — but rebuilds the surface around a clearer information architecture: a persistent navy sidebar, zone-aware topbar, triage-first Ops View, KPI-led Dashboard, and a focused Customer 360° with tabbed detail.
Navy 900 + warm orange 500 · restrained status palette
Poppins · tight letter-spacing for titles
13px base · 56px topbar · 232px sidebar
1280 × 820 artboards · 17 screens
