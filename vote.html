Written Consent of Stockholders
in Lieu of Annual Meeting

Pursuant to Section 228 of the Delaware General Corporation Law and Section 2.14 of the Bylaws of TEKKS Inc. (the “Company”), the undersigned stockholders of the Company, holding not less than the minimum number of votes necessary to authorize the actions set forth below, hereby adopt the following resolutions by written consent in lieu of an annual meeting of stockholders:

1. Election of Directors

WHEREAS, the stockholders desire to elect the Board of Directors of the Company to serve until the next annual meeting of stockholders or until their successors are duly elected and qualified, or until their earlier resignation or removal;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, that each of the following nominees is hereby submitted for election to the Board of Directors of the Company, with the undersigned stockholders voting as indicated below:

  1. Tommaso Esmanech
  2. Neel Srinivasan Karamadai
  3. Sandra Castiblanco
  4. Jennie Martinez

RESOLVED FURTHER, that each nominee receiving the affirmative vote of stockholders holding a majority of the outstanding voting power of the Company shall be elected as a director.

2. Effectiveness of Consent

RESOLVED, that this Written Consent shall be effective as of the date on which stockholders holding the requisite number of shares have executed and delivered this Written Consent to the Company.

3. Ratification of Actions

RESOLVED, that all actions previously taken by the Board of Directors and officers of the Company consistent with the foregoing resolutions are hereby ratified, confirmed, and approved in all respects.

4. General Authorization

RESOLVED, that any officer of the Company is hereby authorized and directed to take all such further actions and to execute and deliver any and all documents as such officer may deem necessary or appropriate to carry out the intent of the foregoing resolutions.

Execution

This Written Consent may be executed in one or more counterparts, including by electronic transmission (including email or electronic signature), each of which shall be deemed an original, and all of which together shall constitute one and the same instrument.

Delivery

This Written Consent shall be delivered to the Company by delivery to its principal place of business or via electronic transmission to an officer of the Company, and shall be filed with the minutes of the proceedings of the stockholders.
